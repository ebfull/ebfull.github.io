In order to achieve zero-knowledge and short proofs, we need the prover to calculate the inner products and send the results to the verifier. However, the prover cannot know the point xxx at which the QAP will be evaluated, or they will be able to find h(x)h(x)h(x) for which the identity holds for any statement.
