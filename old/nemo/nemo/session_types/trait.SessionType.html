pub unsafe trait SessionType {
    type Dual: SessionType;
}

All session types have duality. Two clients that communicate will always have a session type that is the dual of their counterpart.

As an example, the dual of Recv<T, S> is Send<T, S::Dual>. That is, one client expects to receive T and switch to session S, while the other expects to send T and switch to the dual of S.
